What actually is Zhmerynka orchid nursery?

Previous Next

Zhmerynka orchid nursery is placed in Zhmerynka village on street Vyhovskoho 21. We both make orders from far East and grow orchids by ourselves, making the final price of it much cheaper. Also we cooperate with another greenhouses of Ukraine to make our assortment wider and to appeal more clients.
Our nursery is really large - 100X200 square metres and has 3 main sections: orchids, cacti, bromeliacae and other species. The range of sizes and variety of species can excite even an experienced collectioner. Owners of this beautiful place are Ivanna and Robert Matskevich - a couple of orchid- and plant-lovers, florists and just nice people.

Our assortment


Orchids

A great viriety of exclusive, natural genus of phalaenopsis,diversity of vandas, oncidiums, the queen of orchids cattleya, lycastas, catasetums, dendrobiums, big, medium and miniature cymbidiums, hibryds and natural genuses of different orchids, exotic orchids like bulbophyllums, brassias, the perl of Africa - podangis, encyclia, gongora - a jewel orchid, and many others!

Cacti

We have an exquisite cacti collection as well. Cold-loving Schlumbergera, Epihpyllum and Hilocereus won't make you any trouble growing at kind of shady and colder rooms, exotic Mammilaria, Notocactus, Opuncia feel themselves perfect being placed on sunny windowsill with direct sun, tolerate extreme temperatures - both very hot and below zero. Also such beautiful jams as Ferocactus, different kinds of Rebutia and Echinopsis are avalible.

Bromeliaceae

What can be more eyecatching exotic addition to your plants collection than a Bromeliaceae species? Billbergia, Vriesea, Tillandsia have beautiful leafs as well as flower spikes, you'll definatelly enjoy gorgeous beauty of Aechmea and Nidularium! Just come to our nursery and see!
